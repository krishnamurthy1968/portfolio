ABOUT US

Fullinfaws College, an Anglo-Indian institution affiliated to Bangalore University, is run by the Lawrence Fullinfaw Memorial Trust. The trust was formed in 1997 to commemorate the memory of the late Mr. Lawrence Anthony Fullinfaw. Fullinfaws College is an Anglo-Indian Christian Minority Educational Institution. Anglo-Indian institutions are reputed for their discipline, commitment and dedication to the cause of education of the younger generation. This discipline, commitment and dedication is now being extended in the fields of higher education also. Fullinfaws College is the first Anglo-Indian college in the country to provide this service

Ideals which the college believes and shares

Education is an essential requisite for a happy, peaceful and progressive life.

  Education brings human values and material wealth, which in turn lead to Dharma and Happiness in this world.
  Knowledge should have the power to transform an individual and should not just aim for information.
  Theoretical knowledge should be practiced, lightened into wisdom and assimilated into our daily life.
  Knowledge should bring about Truth, Kindness, Compassion, Sacrifice, Respect and Reverence to parents and elders, obedience to teachers and adoration towards God.

The objectives

To encourage these beliefs by sharing them with the students; by providing necessary skills; techniques and experiences, thereby enabling them to successfully compete for good job opportunities in India and abroad.

To enhance the creativity of each student by providing necessary guidance.

To inspire in each student leadership qualities, thereby encouraging them to give their best to themselves, their family, society and to the nation.

To motivate students to achieve excellence, not only in academic pursuits but also in every sphere of human existence.

College plans to achieve these goals

Through its committed, motivated, highly skilled, well qualified and reputed faculty, who will strive for innovation and excellence

Through discipline in a systematic way with well defined responsibility and accountability.

By according top priority for student needs through prompt and appropriate responses.

Through its committed, motivated, highly skilled, well qualified and reputed faculty, who will strive for innovation and excellence

By monitoring the growth of each student through objective evaluation of their performances.

By encouraging optimum utilisation of time and other valuable resources including the library, information centre and the computer laboratory.
