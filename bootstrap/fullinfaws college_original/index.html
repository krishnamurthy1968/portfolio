ABOUT THE COLLEGE

Their Mission and Vision is now our inspiration Late Mr. Lawrence Anthony Fullinfaw, the inspiration behind the institution, strongly advocated the use of education as a means to build the life, career and character of every individual living in our society. As an eminent educationist, Lawrence Anthony Fullinfaw fully believed that through the path of education individuals can face challenges, eradicate poverty and overcome hurdles by finding solutions not only for themselves but also forthewhole society.
